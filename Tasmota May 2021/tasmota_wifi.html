1. If this is not the first time you are setting up WiFi, press the button on your IoT device for about 5 seconds.

2. A Wifi AP of the form intof-xxxx will be activated.

3. Connect your Wifi enabled laptop to the intof-xxxx network.

4. Enter your home router's SSID and Wifi password in the text boxes above and submit.

5. The IoT device will automatically restart and connect to your home router's Wifi network.

6. Connect your laptop back to your home network.

7. If you made a mistake, press the button on the device for 5 seconds. This will reset the Wifi data. Then repeat the above steps.
